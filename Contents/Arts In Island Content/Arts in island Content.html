Art In Island

Adress: The Media Square, Cubao, Quezon City 1100

History:

Arts in Island PH opened its doors in 2014, marking a new era of immersive art experiences in the Philippines. It quickly gained popularity among art enthusiasts and curious visitors alike, becoming the country's first and largest mixed media art museum. The museum's founders envisioned a space where art transcends traditional boundaries, inviting visitors to engage with creativity and imagination in a truly interactive and captivating way.


Facts and Trivia:

Arts in Island PH stands out as a unique and captivating destination for art lovers and curious minds. It is a place where imagination runs wild and creativity knows no bounds. The museum's collection features a diverse range of multimedia artworks, including:

• 3D Paintings: These mesmerizing artworks defy the limitations of traditional painting, creating illusions of depth and dimension that transport viewers into captivating scenes.

• Optical Illusions: Prepare to have your mind tricked and your senses challenged as you navigate through a realm of mind-bending illusions that play on perception and reality.

• Interactive Installations: Engage with the art as you interact with these dynamic installations, triggering sensory experiences that blur the lines between spectator and participant.

• Themed Zones: Immerse yourself in various themed zones, each meticulously crafted to evoke distinct emotions and transport you to different worlds.
