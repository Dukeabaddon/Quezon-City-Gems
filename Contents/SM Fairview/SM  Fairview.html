SM City Fairview

Adress: Quirino Highway, Novaliches, Quezon City

History:

SM City Fairview, located in Quezon City, Philippines, has a rich history dating back to its opening on November 23, 1997. As part of the SM Supermalls chain owned by SM Prime Holdings, it quickly became a prominent shopping destination in the northern part of Metro Manila. The mall underwent expansions to accommodate a growing number of visitors, solidifying its status as a comprehensive retail and entertainment hub. With a diverse array of stores, dining options, and entertainment facilities, SM City Fairview caters to a wide range of preferences and needs. The mall's strategic location in the Fairview area contributes to its popularity, serving as a central gathering place for residents and visitors alike. Over the years, SM City Fairview has evolved, incorporating modern amenities and features to stay relevant in the dynamic retail landscape. Today, it continues to be a vital part of the community, offering a vibrant and dynamic shopping experience to all who visit.


Facts and Trivia:

SM City Fairview is a popular destination for children who delight in playing at Tom's World, particularly their claw machines, as well as exploring other play areas like Kidzoona. On weekends, the mall teems with shoppers, so it's advisable to arrive early to avoid the crowds. Additionally, SM Fairview Hypermarket offers the convenience of online grocery shopping through their user-friendly app. Located by the highway, this spacious mall houses an array of shops and dining establishments.


Additional Information:

SM City Fairview is known for hosting various community events and activities, including health and wellness programs, fitness classes, and cultural celebrations. The mall serves as a hub for local communities to come together, promoting a sense of unity and camaraderie. From fitness challenges to cultural festivals, these events contribute to the overall vibrancy and inclusiveness of SM City Fairview. The mall's commitment to fostering a community spirit goes beyond shopping, providing visitors with opportunities to engage in diverse and enriching experiences.
