SM North Edsa

Adress: North Avenue, corner EDSA, Quezon City

History:

SM City North EDSA, located in Quezon City, Philippines, stands as one of the largest shopping malls in Southeast Asia and a prominent landmark in Metro Manila. It opened its doors on November 25, 1985, as the first SM Supermall and a flagship project of the Sy family's SM Prime Holdings. Originally envisioned by retail magnate Henry Sy Sr., the mall underwent expansions over the years, evolving into a sprawling complex that includes The Block, Annex, Sky Garden, and City Center. Known for its diverse retail offerings, entertainment facilities, and dining options, SM City North EDSA has played a pivotal role in transforming the retail landscape of the Philippines. The mall has witnessed significant developments, including the integration of transportation terminals and the Sky Dome, a venue for concerts and events. As a cultural and commercial hub, SM City North EDSA continues to attract millions of visitors, contributing to the vibrancy of Quezon City and the broader metropolitan area.


Facts and Trivia:

SM City North EDSA has firmly embedded itself in Philippine pop culture, featured prominently in films and television shows, thanks to its iconic structures and lively ambiance, making it a sought-after location for filmmakers. Beyond its primary role as a premier shopping and entertainment destination, the mall has attained cultural significance, with its diverse spaces serving as backdrops for scenes in viewers' favorite movies and TV series. This connection between the mall and the entertainment landscape adds a unique layer of familiarity for visitors. Notably, SM City North EDSA boasts one of the largest indoor ice skating rinks in the Philippines, enhancing the mall experience with a novel and entertaining activity amid the urban hustle. This distinctive feature, popular among families and those seeking a departure from conventional shopping and dining, underscores the mall's commitment to offering diverse and engaging experiences. The ice skating rink, in particular, has become a favored attraction, reflecting the mall's dedication to providing visitors with unique and enjoyable alternatives beyond the typical retail offerings.


Additional Information:

SM City North EDSA is home to the Sky Garden, an expansive rooftop garden that not only provides a relaxing green space amidst the urban setting but also features unique attractions. One notable feature is the Sky Dome, an events venue within the garden that has hosted a variety of concerts, product launches, and other live performances. This innovative use of rooftop space adds an extra dimension to the mall experience, making SM City North EDSA not only a retail destination but also a versatile venue for entertainment and cultural events.
